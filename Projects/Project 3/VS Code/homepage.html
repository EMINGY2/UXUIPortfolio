    • Our Mission

    1. To provide a nurturing and therapeutic home for children
    2. To restore each child to a healthy family setting

    Our Belief

    Each Child deserves a loving and permanent home.


    This simple belief inspires us each day to achieve our goals of protecting and restoring the lives of victimized children so that these children will rediscover the future they can have and reclaim that future.

    Our Goal

    To enrich, educate and empower these children through loving care, professional treatment and innovative services that heal.

    Our Commitment

    Helping Hands Home for Children is committed to the best practices, to constant performance and quality improvement, to evidence-based services and to stakeholder involvement.


    Our National Accreditation through the Council of Accreditation affirms that we are committed to the highest possible standards amongst our peers in Child Welfare, and we are proud to be a part of this Community of Excellence.
