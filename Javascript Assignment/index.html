Who am I?


I am an aspiring UI architect armed with little more than a college education (a major in history, a minor in english), a pen, a piece of paper, and a feel for intuitive design. For most of my life, I've been in food service, bussing tables, doing the dishes, all while aspiring for more. After getting my education and a degree from Texas State, I quickly learned that a lifetime spent in history museums or in classrooms was not the place for me. I enrolled in a boot camp for UX and UI Design seeking new horizons to chase and a different set of skills with which to chase what I desired.

When I'm not writing out long lines of code, I spend my time doing things like bowling and playing my favorite video games. I enjoy spending time playing games like League of Legends, which requires a team of 5 random strangers to work together to achieve the goal of destroying the enemy team's nexus to win the game. How that gets done is purely up to the players and generally speaking, no two games are ever that similar.
